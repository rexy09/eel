Who we are ?

The name of the company “ELIGOD ENTERPRISES LIMITED” was incorporated under the certificate of incorporation No 133561.

We carry on importing and supply of School or Educational materials, scientists equipments and technological accessories such as primary and secondary books, computer accessories, laboratory equipments and chemicals.

We have a vibrant management team assisted by very experienced professionals to deliver on our promises to our clients.

Headed by the founder, Mr. Shoo Godwin and managed by a team of dedicated professionals, It’s reputation for sales and service excellence has been acquired over a period of 5 years. Today, EEL represents a comprehensive yet diversified range of products on behalf of manufacturers within the country.
